My family and I restored a 1972 Ford Bronco

Here's our truck before we restored it.

Rebuilding a 1972 Ford Bronco alongside my dad was an exhilarating journey, filled with cherished moments and invaluable lessons. Over the course of two years, our hands-on effort and persistent dedication ingrained in me the profound essence of "built, not bought." This transformative experience even became the heart of my college essay, a testament to its profound impact on my life.

Now, the Bronco serves as our ticket to sun-soaked drives along the boulevard of Long Beach Island. We relish the scenic views and the feel of the breeze, especially during our leisurely cruises to grab some delectable ice cream at the Skipper Dipper. Each journey reminds us of the hard work put into the vehicle and the simple joys it brings.
